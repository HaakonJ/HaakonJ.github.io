Rapid Prototyping Kids Website

For an assignment I was given two and a half weeks to design and create kids websites. This is the third of four websites I had to design.

I put in the extra effort in creating all, but two of the images used in the kids website. I really wanted to test how much I could do in a short time.

There was an added bonus of an easter egg where if you scrolled over the secret, button the Super Dude character would turn into just a regular guy.
