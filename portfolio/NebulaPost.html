Nebula Graphic Designs

The work done in the next two images was attained from the use of making two different brushes from two different Nebula images. At first, I just wanted to see what I could do by making the red to blue nebula eye image and putting two opposing gradient masks over opposing colored nebula brush images, later I placed the purple nebula in the center.

After doing that I had the idea to use a similar idea to create a Captain America shield by alternating this affect alternating the masked-out areas on different each of the nebula images.
