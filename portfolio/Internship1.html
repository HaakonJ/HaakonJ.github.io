Cascadia Veterans Community Website

For this internship I was asked to update and add some new functionality to the Veterans Benefits page for Cascadia College. I was part of a team of people which created the original prototype of the website from the starting design all the way to the final design shown here.

For the first three months I work with two other people talking with the client at Cascadia to add in several desired functions as well as retouches to basic information on the site. Later I picked it up as an intern and worked to finish it off on my own.

In the final Site there were a total of five pages. There was a general overview launch page, an about page talking about staff, office hours and other basic informaiton. The next two pages were either on important veterans information and a resources page. The last page was a calender page that was designed using JavaScript to automatically update and it was designed to allow you to create buttons the would pop up information on specific events on the calender.

The JavaScript for the calender were all designed by me as well as a large portion of the later updates to the website from the original that happened during my internship. I also was resposible for putting together all of the content that wasn't part of the original site and communicating to the client.
