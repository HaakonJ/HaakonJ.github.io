Clouds

These are a couple of graphic art pieces I created to learn more about how different people draw clouds in comics. I used a couple of different techniques to make these clouds. In the end the original picture was made with a combination of some sketching I did using a Wacom tablet alternating between several hues of blue and changing the size of the brush. Later I created a brush from an image of an actual cloud.

By combining both techniques I could get both the fine detail of the cloud brush as well as the mistier simple clouds on the edges that just kind of hang there.
